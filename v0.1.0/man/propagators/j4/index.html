J4 Analytical Orbit Propagator

The J4 analytical orbit propagator considers the perturbation caused by the terms $J_2$, $J_2^2$, and $J_4$. It is a slightly more precise version of the J2 analytical orbit propagator useful for mission design and analysis.

Algorithm

The algorithm implemented here is based on [1, 2]. This version takes into account only the secular effects in the orbit elements.

Considering only the terms $J_2$, $J_2^2$, and $J_4$, we obtain that only the right accession of the ascending node (RAAN), the argument of perigee, and mean motion are perturbed by the geopotential field with secular effects. However, we also include the possibility of inputting the mean motion time derivatives to consider the impact like energy dissipation by air drag. The complete algorithm is available in [1, pp. 647-654, 692-693] where we assumed $J_6 = 0$.

References

  • [1] Vallado, D. A (2013). Fundamentals of Astrodynamics and Applications. 4th ed. Microcosm Press, Hawthorn, CA, USA.
  • [2] Hoots, F. R., Roehrich, R. L (1980). *Models for Propagation of NORAD Elements Set. **Spacetrack Report No. 3.
